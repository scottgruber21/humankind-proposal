Is this what a social worker does?

Social workers focus on psychosocial and insurance navigation. I provide clinical expertise: I read pathology reports, understand staging, and communicate peer-to-peer with your oncologists .

Do you provide medical treatment?

No. I provide informed guidance and coordination. I am an advocate and clinical navigator, not a treating clinician or diagnostician.

How is liability handled?

I carry professional liability coverage and every engagement is defined by a clear agreement that outlines our scope of work.

What about referral fees?

To maintain total clinical integrity, I do not pay or receive referral fees from any physician or institution.
